# ğŸŒ€ 5. Spatial Glitch Templates

When designing worldspace anomalies, use these templates:

### **Flicker-Shift**
A small part of the scene freezes or jumps a frame.

### **Loop-Cell**
The pawn turns a corner and returns to the same spot but not quite as they left it.

### **Parallax Drift**
Foreground objects move correctly, but background moves wrong.

### **Shadow Desync**
Shadows move out of sync with entities.

### **Wireframe Exposure**
Textures stutter, revealing geometric mesh.

### **Horizon Buckle**
Distant lines bend inward or ripple subtly.

### **Depth Collapse**
Objects appear 2D for a moment.

### **Silent Drop**
All ambient sound stops simultaneously.
