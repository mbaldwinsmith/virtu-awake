## How to use
- Pick the skill + tier base line.
- Replace or append the trait overlay for the pawn's trait at the same tier.
- Mood buffs: Tier 1 = +3, Tier 2 = +6, Tier 3 = +10 (adjust further per trait modifiers in MEMORY_GUIDE).
